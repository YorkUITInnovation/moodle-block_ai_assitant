Excel AutoTest Template Instructions

  1. Open the Excel AutoTest Template: Ensure you have the template open and ready to edit.
  2. Understand the Columns:
    • Section: This column represents the category of the questions.
    • Questions: This column holds the questions to be asked.
    • Answer: This column contains the anticipated answers.
  3. Entering Data:
    • First Question in a Section:
      • Enter the section name in the Section column.
      • Enter the question in the Questions column.
      • Enter the anticipated answer in the Answer column.
    • Additional Questions in the Same Section:
      • Leave the Section column empty.
      • Enter the next question in the Questions column.
      • Enter the anticipated answer in the Answer column.
  4. Example:
Section Questions Answer
Math What is 2+2? 4
What is the square root of 9? 3
Science What is the chemical symbol for water? H2O
What planet is known as the Red Planet? Mars
  1. Review and Save:
    • Double-check your entries for accuracy.
    • Save the template to ensure all your data is preserved.
