Instructions for Using the Syllabus Template

The syllabus template is designed to ensure accuracy and consistency when training the AI bot, AL the Course Assistant. The template consists of placeholders that start with < and end with >. Follow these steps to effectively use the template:

Step 1: Open the Template

  1. Open the syllabus template file in your preferred text editor or word processor.

Step 2: Identify Placeholders

  1. Look for placeholders within the template. These placeholders are enclosed in angle brackets, such as , , etc.

Step 3: Replace Placeholders

  1. Replace each placeholder with the appropriate information. For example:
    • : Enter the title of the course.
    • : Enter the name of the instructor.
    • : Provide a brief description of the course.
    • : Enter the start date of the course.
    • : Enter the end date of the course.
    • : Provide the schedule for the classes, including days and times.
    • : Enter the office hours for the instructor.
    • : Provide contact information for the instructor or course coordinator.

Step 4: Review and Save

  1. Carefully review the filled-in template to ensure all placeholders have been replaced with accurate information.
  2. Save the updated syllabus file with a new name to avoid overwriting the original template.

Step 5: Use the Syllabus

  1. Use the completed syllabus for your course. This document will help ensure that AL the Course Assistant has accurate and consistent information to assist students effectively.
